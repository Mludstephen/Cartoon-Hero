Wreck it, Rango!

About Rango

Rango is the titular hero of "Rango"- An animated movie. Rango is an ordinary chameleon who accidentally winds up in the town of Dirt, a lawless outpost in the Wild West in desperate need of a new sheriff.

Rango in his Sheriff Costume.

Rango's Characteristics:

  1. Rango is a lonely pet lizard with an active imagination
  2. He fancies himself a swashbuckling hero and puts on "shows" with his companions - a headless Barbie, a dead insect, and a wind-up plastic fish.
  3. But when his owner's car makes a harsh turn, Rango's terrarium falls out onto the highway, leaving him utterly alone in the Mojave Desert. After following the advice of a sage armadillo.
  4. Rango comes across a female lizard named Beans, who takes him to her Old West hometown of Dirt, where water is such a scarce commodity that it's kept in a bank.
  5. Rango spins yarn after yarn about being a legendary bandit killer and manages to fell a killer hawk that terrorizes the town, so Dirt's mayor names him sheriff.
  6. But when Rango unintentionally allows the town's water supply to be stolen, he must either find it or admit that he's just a pretender.
